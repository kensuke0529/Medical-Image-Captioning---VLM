Key Strengths
  • Successful adoption of medical terminology and standard reporting phrases
  • Excellent identification of normal anatomical structures
  • Consistent adherence to medical report format
  • Efficient memory utilization within Colab constraints
  • Clear learning progression with stable validation loss
Limitations
  • Limited overall accuracy (30.1% semantic similarity)
  • Tendency toward generic "normal" findings
  • Weak detection of pathological conditions
  • Limited semantic understanding of nuanced findings
  • Performance ceiling at current dataset scale
Recommendations
  • Extend training duration beyond 10 epochs with larger, more diverse medical datasets
  • Implement data augmentation to increase pathological condition variety
  • Conduct domain-specific pre-training on broader medical imaging datasets
  • Develop medical-specific evaluation metrics beyond semantic similarity
  • Focus training on underrepresented pathological cases to reduce normal-finding bias
